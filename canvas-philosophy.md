# Sporttery Analytics

**Philosophy**: Data visualization as monumental form. Bold geometric precision meets sports betting culture. Information architecture through systematic color coding and bold typography. The invisible becomes observable through meticulous chart design.

**Visual Language**:
- Deep navy (#0a1628) as foundation, evoking stadium nights
- Gold (#ffd700) accent for winners and high-value odds
- Clean tabular structure with mathematical precision
- Score odds highlighted in dedicated color zones
- Minimal text, maximum visual hierarchy

**Expression**: Think Bloomberg terminal meets World Cup aesthetics. Clinical data presentation with the gravitas of financial charts, punctuated by the energy of international football.
